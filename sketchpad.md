## Project Details
- **Description:** Marketing site rebuild for Lowcountry Guns & Range, an indoor gun range business.
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, TypeScript
- **Key Features:** Range Information (Rules, Rentals), Memberships, Training Courses, FAQ.
- **Design:** "Bento-box" style, premium feel, dark/tactical aesthetic with high contrast, Inter font.
